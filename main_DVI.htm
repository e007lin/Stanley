TSMC continues to strengthen its Dedicated IC Foundry capacity, process technology and service capabilities to lay a foundation for logic IC innovations. Because of its ability to provide a full array of services, including design and mask making services, TSMC accommodates a wide variety of customer needs at every manufacturing stage. TSMC served about 449 customers and manufactured more than 9,275 products for various applications covering a variety of computer, communications and consumer electronics market segments. The Company's 2016 capital expenditure was approximately US$10.2 billion.
